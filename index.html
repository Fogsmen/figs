About Us

We believe in miracles, and we know how to make them happen!

We add our very own mix of innovation and poetry to the traditional elements of the circus in order to reveal the beauty of life:

Imagination Technology Innocence

To start with, most of our events take place outdoors, preferably in a forest. A circus is a portal to another dimension, in which anything becomes possible... Nature helps our visitors experience the eerie feeling of traveling through dimensions!

Let wild animals be wild...and free! We value animal well-being, and we are also huge AI geeks. Therefore we have developed unique technologies that replicate animal behaviours: WildSentient©, FantasgeekBeasts©, Zoologeek©, to name a few.

This is how we became the first circus to showcase a Unicorn! This Unicorn symbolizes all the values we would like to bring to the world: imagination, innovation and innocence.
